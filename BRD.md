# Business Requirement Document (BRD)

## 1. Objective
To analyze sales performance and identify revenue and profit trends across product categories.

## 2. Business Problem
The organization wants to understand:
- Which product category generates the highest sales?
- Which category generates the highest profit?
- What is the overall business performance?

## 3. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity Sold
- Profit Margin

## 4. Functional Requirements
- Dashboard should display KPI summary.
- Sales comparison by product category.
- Profit comparison by product category.
- Clean visual representation using charts.

## 5. Expected Outcome
Enable decision-makers to identify top-performing categories and improve profitability.
